Hello! I am Kaixing ZHAO and Ph.D. Candidate in Human-Computer Interaction at the Elipse team, Toulouse Institute of Computer Science Research (CNRS UMR 5505), which is a joint lab between CNRS (French National Center for Scientific Research) and University of Toulouse. My research is dedicated to designing novel interaction techniques in the field of ubiquitous computing, cognitive science and assistive technology. I am also interested in artificial intelligence technologies.

My supervisors are Dr. Christophe Jouffrais (Senior Research Scientist in Cognitive Science, CNRS, Director of IPAL Lab of CNRS and Director of "Cherchons pour Voir" Lab) and Dr. Marcos Serrano (Associate Professor in Computer Science, University of Toulouse).
